Sarah Chen

Specialist in Minimalist, Botanical, and Watercolor Tattoos

About Sarah

Sarah Chen is a celebrated tattoo artist known for her elegant minimalist lines and soft botanical themes. With over 6 years of experience in the tattoo industry, Sarah blends traditional ink with watercolor textures to create truly unique pieces.

She believes tattoos should tell a personal story — delicate, timeless, and meaningful.
